# Impact — Vision

**The Growth Operating System**

---

## What Impact Is

Impact is an AI-powered growth infrastructure platform built initially for **ninety two**, a branding agency. It is not only a signal engine—it handles the **full path** from discovery to close:

```
signal found
  → score lead
  → research company
  → personalize angle
  → start nurture
  → answer objections / FAQs
  → qualify budget / timeline / need
  → create ethical urgency
  → book only qualified calls
  → prepare closer brief
  → track deal
  → follow up forever
```

Impact is **not** another CRM. It is **not** Apollo. It is **not** Monday. It is **not** Clay or Intercom or Zapier.

Impact sits **above** those systems and owns the middle layer they do not:

| Tool | Role |
|------|------|
| Apollo | Finds contacts |
| HubSpot / Salesforce | Tracks CRM |
| Clay | Enriches data |
| Intercom | Answers chats |
| Zapier | Connects workflows |
| **Impact** | **Intent → qualification → nurture → urgency → qualified booked call → closer brief** |

It is the internal operating system that makes a small team feel like it has 20 SDRs, 10 researchers, 5 proposal writers, and 3 project coordinators working 24/7.

---

## Mission

Replace repetitive sales development work with an AI-driven growth engine that continuously:

- **Discovers** opportunities from real buying signals
- **Scores** and **qualifies** every opportunity before humans invest time
- **Researches** companies and decision-makers
- **Personalizes** outreach angles from signal context
- **Nurtures** prospects through education, not spam
- **Answers** objections and FAQs before sales calls
- **Creates** ethical urgency when real constraints exist
- **Books** only qualified calls—with full context for closers
- **Tracks** deals and **follows up** indefinitely

**AI eliminates wasted sales time. Humans close qualified opportunities.**

---

## Mindset

> Don't build a lead scraper. Build an operating system for growth.

Every design decision must answer one question:

**Does this help someone go from signal → closed deal with less friction?**

If yes, it belongs in Impact. If no, it does not.

This discipline keeps Impact focused on outcomes, not features. The goal is not to collect leads—it is to create a repeatable, autonomous growth engine that professional services businesses can trust to surface the right opportunities, vet them, nurture them, and hand off only qualified calls to human closers.

## Core Principles

1. **Every opportunity has a next action.** Nothing sits idle. The system always knows what happens next.
2. **Every opportunity receives a score.** Fit, revenue potential, urgency, and confidence are quantified—not guessed.
3. **Every opportunity originates from a real buying signal.** No cold lists. Signals drive the pipeline.
4. **AI performs research.** Humans review and act on synthesized intelligence, not raw data.
5. **AI eliminates wasted sales time. Humans close qualified opportunities.** No unqualified calls reach closers.
6. **Qualification before consultation.** Budget, timeline, need, and decision-maker status are collected or inferred before booking.
7. **Nurture before pressure.** Education and value-first messaging—not cold blasts or false urgency.
8. **Ethical urgency only.** Real constraints (deadlines, capacity, risk)—never manufactured scarcity.
9. **System must be modular.** Each module and engine has one job. Everything plugs into a shared core.
10. **Everything should eventually become autonomous.** Manual steps today become automated workflows tomorrow.

## Long-Term Vision (SaaS)

Impact starts as the growth engine for ninety two. The architecture is **SaaS-first**: multi-tenant, configurable, and market-agnostic from day one.

**Target market:**

- Agencies (branding, creative, marketing)
- Consultants
- MSPs (managed service providers)
- Web firms
- Creative studios
- Professional service businesses

Every business that sells expertise faces the same problem: they need a repeatable way to discover, qualify, nurture, and convert opportunities. Impact solves that at the **operating-system level**—not the tool level.

If executed correctly, Impact becomes one of the most valuable assets in the professional services market: the backbone of how agencies grow—and potentially how thousands of them grow.

## Success Metrics

Impact succeeds when:

- **Signal-to-qualified-call conversion** increases without adding headcount
- **Unqualified calls booked** decreases measurably
- **Time from signal to first nurture touch** decreases
- **Pipeline quality** improves (higher A/B grades, higher close rates)
- **BD team capacity** increases without burnout
- **Every A-grade opportunity** has a closer brief before the call
- **Nurture reactivation** converts C-grade leads to A/B over time
- **Daily briefings** replace status meetings
- **Objection handling** happens before humans get on calls
